Complete Hybrid Cost-Estimation Formula

EstimatedBill = ( Σ (Poweri × Hoursi × Daysi × LoadFactori) / 1000 + ClusterEnergy )
    × ComponentRates × CorrectionFactor − Discounts
Poweri — appliance rated power (W)
Hoursi — average hours used per day
Daysi — number of usage days within the billing cycle
LoadFactori — duty-cycle or efficiency adjustment (refrigerators, inverter aircons)
ClusterEnergy — energy computed for grouped low-power devices (charging & lighting)
ComponentRates — per-kWh rates derived from the scanned Meralco bill (Generation, Transmission, Distribution, System Loss, Taxes, Universal Charges, FIT-All, GEA-All)
CorrectionFactor — adaptive calibration multiplier from previous billing cycles
Discounts — applicable Senior Citizen and Lifeline deductions
